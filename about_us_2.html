Our Objectives

We are a young, dynamic and flexible organization that is going to consolidate its position in the global market as a leading multi-skilled services provider in the fields of software development, translations and geology. We are going places because with us, the sky is no limit ! We are independant Programmers, Project Managers, Geologists & Translators based in Florence, Italy. The founder started his working career compiling geological databases in South Africa for 1 year. He then worked as a mining and exploration Geologist for Anglo American mining company on various coal and CBM projects and mines in Southern Africa for 2 years. He moved onto engineering geology where he was involved in geotechnical investigations for various land development projects in Southern Africa for 2 years. It was during the Mozal Aluminium Smelter project that he met his Italian wife and moved to Italy where he had a brief stint in petroleum exploration for Agip and Total for 2 years. He became Microsoft certified in Visual Basic in 2001 and has since been working as a programmer in Florence for various software houses and companies. Currently he is a Project Manager involved in IT infrastructure and SW development projects for over 3 years and is a Prochain and PMP certified Project Professional.
