Websites for professionals and companies are constructed, published and managed using up to date technologies and tools. Webmasters first consult their clients to determine the needs and the target audience of the client. This is essential for effective web design.

Web Promotion is the next logical step. Having a website is pointless unless it can be found by means of an internet search.  Our tools automate all the necessary tasks to achieve a top 10 ranking of our client's web pages in all the major search engines. Firstly, the Reporter reports on the current status of a website as determined by the search engines. After this, action can be taken to improve the rankings using the Page Generator which optimizes web pages. The Page Critic will then analyze these generated pages or existing pages to advise us on how to further fine-tune them in order to optimize their content to rank higher on each major search engine. Once the pages are ready, WebPosition uploads them to the website. The Submitter then automatically submits all optimized web pages from the website to all of the major search engines. The Traffic Analyzer reports traffic to each page and what keywords were used to find them. We then go back and re-run the Reporter to monitor our client's progress over time. This process guarantees success.
